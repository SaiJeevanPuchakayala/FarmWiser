About FarmWiser


Introducing FarmWiser, the ultimate farmer's companion that provides expert guidance on all crop-related matters. With cutting-edge features which includes a Weather Forecaster, Crop Recommendation System, and Realtime Soil Health Monitoring and Alerting System: Soilitix, this app ensures that farmers stay ahead of the game with advance knowledge of atmospheric conditions and soil health. And that's not all! FarmWiser also offers a State Wise agriculture Commodity Price Viewer, Plant Disease Detection System: ChromaticScan, and agriculture News Viewer that extracts relevant information from multiple media sources into an easy-to-understand format. Say goodbye to uncertainty and hello to a brighter, more prosperous future with FarmWiser.
